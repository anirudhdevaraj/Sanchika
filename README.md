🌐 **Website:** [https://sanchika.ciil.org](https://sanchika.ciil.org)

---

## About

**Bhasha Sanchika** is a comprehensive digital archive and language repository developed by the Central Institute of Indian Languages (CIIL), established under the Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Government of India. It serves as a dynamic platform for the preservation, dissemination, and promotion of Indian languages and linguistic heritage.

CIIL, founded in 1969, plays a central role in language development and research across India. The institute advises and assists Central and State Governments on language-related matters and promotes learning of **20 Indian languages** among non-native speakers.


## Core Objectives

| Objective | Description |
|---|---|
| **Preservation** | Ensuring the long-term storage of linguistic and cultural data |
| **Digital Presence** | Enhancing the visibility of Indian languages and mother tongues on the web |
| **Diverse Formats** | Resources available in text, image, audio, and video formats |
| **Language Technology** | Creating technology resources and pedagogy materials for all Indian languages |

## Specialized Sections

### 📁 Bharatavani
A multilingual knowledge repository covering Indian languages and cultures.

### 📁 Bhasha Mandakini
A specialized linguistic resource collection under CIIL.

### 📁 CIIL Publications
Official publications and research outputs from the Central Institute of Indian Languages.

### 📁 CIIL Visual Archive
A visual documentation archive covering Indian linguistic and cultural contexts.

### 📁 Classical Languages
Dedicated resources for India's classical languages.

### 📁 National Tape Archive
Archived audio recordings of Indian languages and dialects.

### 📁 Policy Documents
Language-related policy documents from government and institutional sources.

### 📁 SPPEL — Scheme for Protection and Preservation of Endangered Languages
Resources focused on endangered mother tongues and languages, including raw audio files, transcriptions, translations, photographs, and videos.

## Getting Started

### Browse the Archive
Visit [https://sanchika.ciil.org](https://sanchika.ciil.org) and explore resources using the **Browse** menu:
- By Author
- By Title
- By Subject
- By Language
- By Media
- By Linguistic Data Type

### Create an Account
New users can register at [https://sanchika.ciil.org/register](https://sanchika.ciil.org/register) to access full features and contribute to the repository.

### Contribute
Have rare manuscripts, audio recordings, or linguistic expertise? [Partner with us](https://sanchika.ciil.org/info/feedback) to enrich the archive and support the preservation of Indian languages.
